Get Started

Need to look at all your network data?

PacketStream has you covered. Everything that has happened on your hosts so far, On a neat table. Just filter away whatever you need to find.

Prefer a quick glance of your log?

Just open dataGlance and get a simple set of tables with usually requested data, automatically generated. No need for complicated SQL.

Want to roll your own solution?

Just request data to the system API. If you can parse a JSON, you can roll your own app. All the power of the PCAPAnalyzer system, without the need for the frontend.
